RouteCoin Concept
Let's talk Decentralized Autonomous Organizations in Telecommunications

Mobile Access Everywhere

RouteCoin enables mobiles to connect to any cellular mobile system in any country and any company without requiring mutual agreement between mobile service providers for roaming.

WiFi Access Everywhere

RouteCoin enables computers to connect to any password-enabled WiFi in any location in the world. There is no need to worry about WiFi passwords.

IoT Access

RouteCoin enables IoT devices from different companies to help each other to access Internet, or receiving their new firmware without accessing their company's web servers on Internet.


RouteCoin Concept

RouteCoin lets telecommunication systems to communicate without any need to establish a trust relationship between systems, reduce the role of system administrators from a trust-point to a coordinator, and lets systems provides service to more users without knowing them.
